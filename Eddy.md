# **Eddy Counterplay Cheat Sheet for Tekken 8**

---

### **Overview**

**Stances:** RLX (Relaxed), HSP (Handstand), Special Movements

**General Strategies:**

- **Recognize Stance Entries**
- **Use Ground-Hitting & Anti-Air Moves**
- **Maintain Optimal Range**
- **Punish Unsafe Moves**
- **Be Wary of Mix-ups**
- **Sidestep Left When Appropriate**
